# Account Page User Guide

## Quick Start Guide for Users

### Accessing Your Account Page

#### On Desktop
1. Log in to your account
2. Click the **Account** button in the top-right corner (shows your name or "Account")
3. In the dropdown menu, click **"My Profile"**
4. You'll be taken to your Account page

#### On Mobile
1. Log in to your account
2. Tap the **menu icon** (â˜°) in the top-right corner
3. Scroll down to find **"My Profile"**
4. Tap to open your Account page

## What You Can Do on the Account Page

### View Your Account Information
- **Email Address:** Your registered email
- **Account Role:** Your role(s) in the system (Customer, Runner, or Admin)
- **Member Since:** When you joined the platform

### Edit Your Profile
You can update the following information:
- **First Name** (required)
- **Last Name** (required)
- **Email** (required)
- **Phone Number** (optional)

### Role-Specific Information

#### For Customers
You'll see:
- **Daily Limit:** Maximum amount you can request per day
- **Used Today:** Amount you've already requested today
- **Available Today:** How much you can still request
- **Last Reset:** When your daily limit was last reset

#### For Runners
You'll see:
- **Monthly Earnings:** Your total earnings this month
- **Account Status:** Whether your account is approved

#### For Admins
You'll see:
- **Admin Badge:** Indicating your admin privileges
- Access to all standard profile features

## How to Update Your Profile

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Navigate to Account Page**
   - Click "Account" â†’ "My Profile" in the header

2. **Edit Your Information**
   - Click in any field to edit
   - Update your first name, last name, email, or phone

3. **Save Your Changes**
   - Click the **"Save Changes"** button at the bottom
   - Wait for the confirmation message

4. **Confirmation**
   - You'll see a green notification: "Profile updated successfully!"
   - Your changes are now saved

### Important Notes
- **Required Fields:** First name, last name, and email must be filled in
- **Email Format:** Must be a valid email address (e.g., name@example.com)
- **Phone Number:** Optional - you can leave it blank if you prefer

## Common Questions

### Q: Why can't I change my role?
**A:** Your role (Customer, Runner, Admin) is assigned by administrators and cannot be changed by users for security reasons.

### Q: What happens if I enter an invalid email?
**A:** The system will show an error message: "Please enter a valid email address" and won't save your changes until you fix it.

### Q: Can I leave the phone number blank?
**A:** Yes! The phone number is optional. You can leave it blank or fill it in - it's up to you.

### Q: How do I go back to my dashboard?
**A:** Click the **"Back"** button at the top-left of the Account page. It will take you to:
- **Customers:** My Orders page
- **Runners:** My Deliveries page
- **Admins:** Admin Dashboard

### Q: Will my changes appear immediately?
**A:** Yes! After you save, your profile updates immediately throughout the app. You'll see your new name in the header right away.

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
**A:** No problem! Just go back to the Account page and update your information again. You can change it as many times as you need.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Profile updated successfully!" but I don't see changes
**Solution:** Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R). If the problem persists, log out and log back in.

### Problem: Save button is disabled
**Solution:** Make sure you've filled in all required fields (first name, last name, email) and that your email is in the correct format.

### Problem: Error message appears when saving
**Solution:** 
1. Check that all required fields are filled in
2. Verify your email format is correct (name@example.com)
3. Try again in a few moments
4. If the problem continues, contact support

### Problem: Can't find the Account page
**Solution:**
- **Desktop:** Look for the "Account" button in the top-right corner
- **Mobile:** Tap the menu icon (â˜°) and scroll down to "My Profile"
- Make sure you're logged in

## Tips for Best Experience

### Keep Your Information Up to Date
- Update your email if it changes so you don't miss important notifications
- Add your phone number for better communication
- Keep your name accurate for delivery verification

### Security Best Practices
- Don't share your account credentials
- Log out when using shared devices
- Keep your email secure

### For Customers
- Check your daily limit regularly
- Monitor your usage to plan requests
- Note when your limit resets

### For Runners
- Keep your profile complete for better trust
- Check your monthly earnings regularly
- Ensure your contact information is current
