## How to Access Your Profile

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click/tap the â˜° menu icon** in the top-right corner
2. The menu slides open from the right
3. **Click/tap "My Profile"** at the top of the menu
4. You're taken to your profile page
5. The menu automatically closes

### What You Can Do on Your Profile Page

- View your account information (email, role, member since)
- Edit your personal details:
  - First Name
  - Last Name
  - Email
  - Phone Number
- View role-specific information:
  - **Customers:** Daily limit, usage, available balance
  - **Runners:** Monthly earnings, approval status
  - **Admins:** Role badges
- Save your changes with the "Save Changes" button

## How to Log Out

### Step-by-Step

1. **Click/tap the â˜° menu icon** in the top-right corner
2. The menu slides open from the right
3. **Scroll to the bottom** of the menu
4. **Click/tap "Log Out"** (shown in red)
5. A confirmation dialog appears: "Are you sure you want to log out?"
6. **Click/tap "Log Out"** again to confirm
7. You're logged out and redirected to the login page

### Why the Confirmation?

We ask you to confirm before logging out to prevent accidental logouts. This ensures you don't lose your place if you clicked the button by mistake.

## Closing the Menu

There are several ways to close the menu without taking action:

### Method 1: Click/Tap Outside
- Click or tap anywhere outside the menu
- The menu slides closed

### Method 2: Press Escape (Desktop/Laptop)
- Press the **Esc** key on your keyboard
- The menu closes immediately

### Method 3: Swipe (Mobile/Tablet)
- Swipe the menu to the right
- The menu slides closed

## Common Questions

### Q: Where did the Account button go?
**A:** We replaced it with the universal hamburger menu (â˜°) for a more consistent experience across all devices. The menu now contains everything that was in the Account button, plus more!

### Q: Why is the menu always visible, even on desktop?
**A:** For consistency! Now you have the same navigation experience whether you're on your phone, tablet, or computer. No more hunting for different buttons on different devices.

### Q: Can I still see my navigation links on desktop?
**A:** Yes! On desktop, you'll see your main navigation links in the center of the header (like "Request Cash", "My Orders", etc.). The hamburger menu provides additional options like Profile and Logout.

### Q: What if I have multiple roles?
**A:** No problem! The menu will show sections for each of your roles. For example, if you're both an Admin and a Runner, you'll see both the Admin section and the Runner section in your menu.

### Q: Is the menu accessible for keyboard users?
**A:** Absolutely! You can:
- Press **Tab** to navigate through menu items
- Press **Enter** or **Space** to activate a button
- Press **Esc** to close the menu
- The menu traps focus, so you won't accidentally tab outside

### Q: Is the menu accessible for screen reader users?
**A:** Yes! The menu includes:
- Proper ARIA labels
- Descriptive button text
- Clear heading structure
- Announced state changes

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Menu won't open
**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're clicking/tapping the â˜° icon
2. Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Try a different browser

### Problem: Menu opens but I can't click anything
**Solution:**
1. Make sure the page has fully loaded
2. Try closing and reopening the menu
3. Refresh the page
4. Check your internet connection

### Problem: Profile link doesn't work
**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're logged in
2. Try clicking the link again
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. If problem persists, contact support

### Problem: Logout doesn't work
**Solution:**
1. Make sure you confirm the logout in the dialog
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try refreshing the page
4. If problem persists, contact support

### Problem: Menu is cut off on mobile
**Solution:**
1. Try rotating your device
2. Zoom out if you've zoomed in
3. Refresh the page
4. Update your browser to the latest version
